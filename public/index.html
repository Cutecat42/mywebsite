About Me

I didn't start my career in tech. For years, I worked in customer support roles where I learned how to really listen, solve problems calmly, and help people even when things were stressful. Eventually, I realized that what I loved most was understanding how things worked and fixing the root cause — not just the symptoms.

That curiosity led me to software development. I completed the Springboard Software Engineering Bootcamp and continued teaching myself through personal projects, documentation deep-dives, and way too many late-night debugging sessions.

Today, I build clean, user-centered applications that make people's lives easier. And I'm still always learning — because I genuinely enjoy the process of getting better.
